features

Permissionless

The vaults are open to public and anyone can build his own liquidity management strategies on top of Mellow.

Multi-token

The protocol is designed to work simultaneously with multiple types of tokens within the same strategy.

Composable

In return for provided capital, users get Mellow Vault LP tokens that can be utilized by other protocols.

Cross-protocol

Mellow Vaults system can be easily integrated with any protocol and enables building cross-protocol strategies.

How it works

Read on Medium

The Vault contracts hold the tokens and rebalance them both inside other protocols and between them. Strategy contracts interact with Vault contracts defining the rebalancing parameters.

Liquidity provider

Users pick a strategy that fits their needs and allocate their assets into a vault to earn yield. When the assets are deposited, users get composable LP tokens (ERC-20).

Strategies

Strategies are smart-contracts that implement the models to provide effective liquidity allocation. Different market events can trigger the strategies to initiate rebalance.

Vaults

Vaults allocate multiple ERC-20 tokens into other DeFi protocols and rebalance the liquidity in accordance with Strategies inside and between the protocols.

Our vision

Decentralized markets efficiency

Sophisticated math models are implemented via smart-contracts to create a practical set of tools for the service of the DeFi ecosystem. Our goal is to eliminate market inefficiency and increase composability of DeFi.

Mellow team believes in synergy between protocols and focuses on composability to provide new tools both to users and other teams.
